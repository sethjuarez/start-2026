# Outline

## Segment 1 – Build plotline + our advantage 

###  Beat 1 – The platform shift (echo Build intro) 

- Hi, I'm Kyle Daigle. GitHub COO, CMO of Marketing at Microsoft, but most importantly I'm a developer. 
- It's both an exciting and confusing time to be a developer. 
- In less than two years time we've moved from assist to autonomous agents. 
- That creates real pressure. Pressure to build fast. AND operate, optimize and observe your workflows. THEN, there’s securing your infrastructure, apps, and agents seamlessly in a way that doesn't slow you down. 

### Beat 2 – The duality of the modern developer  

- There's always been this duality in being a developer. You’re a tinkerer, choosing your own tools and models, and you’re an enterprise builder, shipping systems that demand governance, security and trust from day one. 
- Developers don’t need another way to just build and run an agent or app. They need trust. They need native context and knowledge. Most of all, they need choice to access the right model for the right problem. 
- Unlike our competitors, this duality is where Microsoft thrives. An end-to-end platform that's model-diverse, open, and heterogeneous at every layer — bringing together what you know with what the world knows. 
- Our competitors? They have just one slice of the pie.  

### Beat 3 – Our advantage 

- Let’s break down our ecosystem advantage.  
- First, we’ve got one hell of a hand. 210 million developers on GitHub. 51 million developers open VS Code every week. 26 million GitHub Copilot users. 12 million on Visual Studio. No competitor starts from this position. 
- And we're not just leading on reach — we're leading on usage. GitHub Copilot is the #1 AI dev tool by active usage. When developers get to choose, they choose us. 
- Now layer on that agentic evolution that so many companies are tussling with right now – going from building to orchestrations to securing to production. We are the only vendor with a production-ready agentic story with the proof and scale to back it up. 
- And all of it runs on an open, secure platform: 11,000+ models through Azure AI Foundry, wrapped in the governance, identity, and security customers expect at enterprise scale. We present choice without the tradeoffs. 

## Segment 2 – Marketing Compete (2.5 mins) 

Goal: sellers leave knowing who they’ll meet and the one counter-move for each. Frame as a 2x2 the field will actually encounter, not a feature war.  

- Here’s what our advantage looks like in the field. You’ll meet five key challengers. Each one is good at one slice. None of them can do what we do across the whole lifecycle.” 
- Cursor gives you a slick editor with great repo indexing — and that's where it stops. We take you IDE → repo → CI → deploy → governed, at enterprise scale. They own the editor; we own the lifecycle. 
- Claude Code locks you into one great model. We run Claude and 11,000 others inside one open platform — with grounding and governance built in. Model choice, not model lock-in. 
- Codex gives you a strong model. We give you the platform around any model — full lifecycle, enterprise trust. A model is not a platform. 
- Cognition sells autonomy. We sell autonomy with control — sandboxes, agentic code review, identity and governance so you stay in command. 
- Amazon and Google lock you to one cloud and their own models. We're open and heterogeneous — any model, any layer, meeting the developer where they already work.
